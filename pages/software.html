Catalyst Orchestrator

Catalyst Orchestrator is a modern, web-based automation control software that is as modular as our hardware. Modern labs struggle to coordinate complex, multi-vendor automation systems, leading to inefficiencies, downtime, and excessive troubleshooting. Catalyst Orchestrator's dynamic scheduling and streamlined automation management allow teams to focus on science, not infrastructure. It's future-ready and built for seamless integration with AI and ML for programmatic work design and execution.
