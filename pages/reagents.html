Cell Free Protein Synthesis Kits

Our's cell free protein synthesis (CFPS) kits are E. coli transcription/translation systems optimized to produce proteins from a T7 RNA Polymerase promoter. Reactions consist of a bacterial cell lysate and a reagent mix that contains all components required for protein production.
